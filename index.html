Research interests

I am interested in how customized designs meet various developmental cognitive issues and challenges. Particularly, I would like to further my studies in how children learn in a diverse cultural environment. Currently, in my cognitive learning design research, I have created a math gadget called “Magic Zero” to teach children how to conceptualize zero.


Areas of Interest

Learning Sciences, Cognitive-sciences Embodiment, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning, Mobile Programming Development, Computer Modeling of Human Cognition, Design-Based Learning, Robotics, Multimedia Production,, Game Development
