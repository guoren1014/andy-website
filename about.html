About Me

I am a young learning scientist and researcher. I grew up in a family with internationally diverse perspectives. I learned English alongside Chinese, won the local chess championship, acted on stage, and (since I was ten years old) have participated in the media production field. I have been an English teacher since my junior year of college. Right now, I am completing my master’s degree in Instructional Design. I have been producing multimedia products in my studio with my teammates, as well as immersing myself in cutting-edge technology, cognitive science, and education. On top of all these, a lot of my attention has been focused on inventing gadgets to help teach math to young children.
